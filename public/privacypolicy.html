BioMetRx Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2026

Health Data

BioMetRx reads step count data from Health Connect to display your daily activity alongside your other health metrics. This data is:

  • Stored only in your personal Supabase account, protected by row-level security
  • Never shared with third parties
  • Never used for advertising
  • Accessible only by you when logged in

What We Read

BioMetRx requests read-only access to:

  • Steps — daily step count from Health Connect

We do not write any data to Health Connect.

Data Storage

Your health readings are stored securely in Supabase with row-level security policies ensuring only you can access your own data.

Data Deletion

You can delete your account and all associated data at any time from the Profile page within the app.

Contact

For questions about this policy, contact us through the app's support channel.
